The Problem: Competitors Are Stealing Your Traffic

Right now, when customers search for Daphne's products or visit your product pages, competitors are showing up in the sponsored ad slots. Every click they capture is a sale you're losing. Without defensive campaigns, you're essentially giving away your hard-earned brand recognition.

What's Happening on Your Product Pages Right Now

Competitor in YOUR Sponsored Slot A customer is looking at YOUR Daphne's Headcover - but a competitor (FurryVerde) is showing in the sponsored product slot on the right. One click and they're gone.
"Customers Also Bought" - All Competitors These slots should show YOUR other products. Instead, competitors are cross-selling to YOUR customers. With defensive campaigns, you own these placements.
Related Products & Display Ads - Competitor Territory The entire "Related products with free delivery" section is competitors. Plus display ads from completely unrelated brands (Peloton!). This is valuable real estate you should own.

The Opportunity

Sales are driven by two factors: Sessions (traffic) and Conversion Rate. With optimized listings in place and premium pricing established, the path to growth is clear: stop competitors from hijacking your traffic and keep customers in your product ecosystem.

Defensive PPC: Secure Your Brand

Run full-funnel defensive campaigns to block competitors and optimize ad spend.

Sponsored Product Defense

Target ASINs within your own collection and bid on brand terms to keep competitors from dominating your listings. These appear on search results and product detail pages.

Sponsored Brand Defense

Run brand-specific headline ads that showcase your logo and multiple products. Captures shoppers searching "Daphne's" before they see competitor options.

Sponsored Display Defense

Retarget shoppers who viewed your products and protect your detail pages from competitor display ads. Keeps customers in your ecosystem.

Quick Setup Advantage

Use broad match on brand terms to secure presence quickly. Defensive campaigns are simple to launch and typically deliver the highest ROAS in your account.

Full-Funnel Brand Defense Campaigns

  • Sponsored Products - Exact match on "Daphne's" and product-specific brand terms
  • Sponsored Brand - Headline banner capturing brand searches
  • Sponsored Brand Video - Video ads on brand search results
  • Product Targeting - Your products showing on your own detail pages
  • Sponsored Display - Retargeting + detail page defense
